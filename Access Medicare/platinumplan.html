ACCESS MEDICARE Platinum Plan

If you have Medicaid or receive Extra Help (LIS) to help pay for your prescription drugs, the Platinum plan may be the plan for you.  Benefits can include:

  • $0 co-pay for PCP and specialist visits
  • $0 co-pay for preventive dental care
  • Up to $1000 per year for comprehensive dental benefits
  • Up to $720 for over-the-counter non-prescription drug and health-related items ($60 per month)
  • $0 co-payments for eyeglasses and contact lenses (1 pair each, every year)
  • Up to $500 for hearing aids (1 every 3 years)
  • Worldwide Emergency Coverage
  • Car service to and from plan-approved locations at NO COST (up to 12 times per year, 6 every six months)
  • $39.70 monthly plan premium (may be paid by Medicaid if you are enrolled in Medicaid)
